## Overview

On any page where we show a map, controls for the map and associated settings and actions will be in the control bar. On most pages this means your catchment settings (drivetime/radius and range), the metric selector (that determines which metric is showing on the map), the population projection control, and where relevant the button to save your current view, or for example to switch into Catchment Edit mode on a site.

You can close the Control Bar if you want a cleaner workspace - just click the sliders icon to open it again.
