# Map

# Panels

The Demographics tab shows population, affluence, staffing, and regional context for your selected site.

<Frame>
  ![Demographics Map 01](/images/demographics-map-01.jpg)
</Frame>

The map stays linked to the site catchment while the panels below update based on your selected area.

## Population panel

<Frame>
  ![Demographics Population 01](/images/demographics-population-01.jpg)
</Frame>

The Population panel combines key population cards with a chart view.

You can click each metric card to switch the chart between total population and age-focused projections.

## Affluence panel

<Frame>
  ![Demographics Affluence 01](/images/demographics-affluence-01.jpg)
</Frame>

The Affluence panel shows core indicators such as average house price, income, and deprivation rank, alongside home ownership split.

## Staffing and NSSEC

<Frame>
  ![Demographics Staffing NSSEC 01](/images/demographics-staffing-nssec-01.jpg)
</Frame>

This section shows registered nurse and care assistant metrics, an NSSEC breakdown table, and an NSSEC distribution chart.

## LADs in catchment

<Frame>
  ![Demographics LADs 01](/images/demographics-lads-01.jpg)
</Frame>

This table lists the LADs inside your current catchment and shows key demographic and supply/planning context metrics for each LAD.

## Region demographics

<Frame>
  ![Demographics Region 01](/images/demographics-region-01.jpg)
</Frame>

The Region Demographics table gives a wider benchmark view beyond the site-level catchment.
