The Planning tab shows planning applications around your selected site.

<Frame>
  ![Planning Map 01](/images/planning-map-01.jpg)
</Frame>

The map shows planning applications near your site location.

Hover your mouse over a hexagon to see a quick summary, then click through to open the full planning application record.

## Planning filters

<Frame>
  ![Planning Filters 01](/images/planning-filters-01.jpg)
</Frame>

Underneath the map is the Planning filter bar, where you can narrow the list to match the applications you want to review.

## Planning list

<Frame>
  ![Planning List 01](/images/planning-list-01.jpg)
</Frame>

Below the filters is the Planning List, showing key details for each application in your selected area.

### Planning list: metric view

<Frame>
  ![Planning Metrics 01](/images/planning-metrics-01.jpg)
</Frame>

Clicking on **Metrics** above the list switches the table to a metric-focused view so you can compare records more quickly.

### Planning list: key competitors

<Frame>
  ![Planning Key Competitors 01](/images/planning-key-competitors-01.jpg)
</Frame>

You can mark applications as key competitors directly from the list to track them alongside your wider site analysis.

### Planning list: download to CSV

<Frame>
  ![Planning CSV Export 01](/images/planning-csv-export-01.jpg)
</Frame>

You can download the Planning List to CSV for external analysis and reporting.
