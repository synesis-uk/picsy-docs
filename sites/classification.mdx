---
title: "Classification"
---

The classification tab breaks your catchment down into individual groups of the predominant population types.

<Frame>
  ![Classification Close 01 1](/images/classification-close-01-1.jpg)
</Frame>

This gives you a different perspective on opportuity volume and also surfaces the profile of the entire population.

Click on an hex on the map to see the top 5 groups by population for that location.

Underneath the map you see the highlighted groups in a list, and under that the full list of all groups in your site's catchment.

<Frame>
  ![Classification Lists 01 1](/images/classification-lists-01-1.jpg)
</Frame>

The data reacts to the Population Projection (in the Control Bar) so you can see estimated population across the groups for any projection year.
