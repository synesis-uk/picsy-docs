The 3D View tab shows an interactive Google 3D map centered on your selected site.

## 3D map view

<Frame>
  ![3D View Map 01](/images/3d-view-map-01.jpg)
</Frame>

You can move around the site in 3D to inspect nearby context, surrounding buildings, and local terrain.

## Data layers in 3D

<Frame>
  ![3D View Layers 01](/images/3d-view-layers-01.jpg)
</Frame>

The 3D scene can show mapped supply, planning, and transport context layers around the site.

## Mini map and orientation

<Frame>
  ![3D View Minimap 01](/images/3d-view-minimap-01.jpg)
</Frame>

A mini map helps you keep orientation while navigating the immersive view.
