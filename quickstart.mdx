### Add a Site

1. From the [Sites](https://picsy.uk/sites) page, type an address or postcode into the search box (top left over the map). The map will zoom to that location.
2. Moving the mouse over the map will now highlight the plots for the visible properties. Click one to select it and choose "Create new site". This creates a new site (there will be a short delay as we set up demographics and drivetimes from the specific location.
3. The page will redirect to the new site page and show the site's [demographics tab](https://docs.picsy.uk/sites/demographics).

### Run a Market Finder search

1. From the [Market Finder](https://picsy.uk/map/market-finder) page, select one or more Local Authority Districts by clicking them on the map
2. In the control bar, choose your preferred catchment settings - drivetime/radius and time/distance, and click 'Search'
3. You will see search results for the selected regions - on this page the hexagons are coloured according to the metrics for _a catchment from that location_, hence showing the relative quality
