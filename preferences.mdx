title: "Preferences"
---

The [Preferences](https://picsy.uk/settings/preferences) page allows you customise how you want to use PICSy's tools. These are the **defaults which will load in for a new site** - when you save a site it saves all of the settings below specific to that site, so the next time you load it will have whatever settings you saved it with.

## Map

### Map Style

Choose the style for the map - options are the default coloured map, a monochrome map which gives more contrast to PICSy's overlays, and Satellite.

### Metric

Choose the metric that shows in the demographics tab when a site page first loads.

### Catchment Model

Choose the default [catchment model](/core/catchment-models) that is used when a site page first loads.

### Comparison Area

Choose which type of comparison you want a site to load in with - choose between Catchment Model, Immediate Area, home LAD (the Local Authority your site is in), LADs in Catchment (an aggregation of the demographics across all Local Authorities your catchment touches), or Region (the region your site is in, so South East, North West etc).

## Filters

Thes are preferences for how a new site will filter Supply and Planning views - selections here will be applied when your site page loads, so for example if you choose 40 minimum beds in the Supply section, your site view will load on showing only care homes with 40 or more beds.

Those filters can still be changed on the individual page. For more information on Filters read [this page](/core/filters).
