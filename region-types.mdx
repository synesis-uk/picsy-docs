## Overview

PICSy's location, search and comparison tools all rely on regional boundaries - these determine catchment comparison metrics, population projections, reference to local plan policy

## Which Region Types does PICSy use?

PICSy uses perdominantly uses Local Authority districts and  Built-Up Areas for local metrics and comparisons. Where relevant arger regions (South East, North West and so on).

## PICSy's "Immediate Area"

When you are looking at a site, PICSy also creates an arbitrary 'Immediate Area', which comprises the hexagon where your site is located, and the ring of hexagons around that central hex, to give you a comparison metric to measure your full catchment against and to see the demographics within walking distance of the dite
