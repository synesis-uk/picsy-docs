---
title: "Map Layers"
---

There's a lot of information on the map views, often showing data on distinct but related data sources - hex metrics, dots for supply and planning locations, nearby bus stops or train stations, overlays for areas affected by planning constraints, and so on.

Rather than give you one long list of everything, we have split those layers across the different 'tab' views (Demographics, Supply, Planning, Location and so on). But even within those tabs the map can get busy and you may not need to see everything at once. Each layer on any view can be toggled on/off using the 'Layers' selector in the top right corner of the map.
